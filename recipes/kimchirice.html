Kimchi Fried Rice

When I tell you this recipe is extremely simple to make, I'm not kidding. It's no surprise that fried rice is a staple meal amongst college students, as it is a breeze to prepare and relatively low-cost for anyone on a budget. Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. As a homemade dish, fried rice is typically made with ingredients left over from other dishes, leading to the emergence of countless variations. The Korean version of fried rice is called bokkeum-bap, and the one showcased here is a popular variety prepared with kimchi, called kimchi bokkeum-bap. Kimchi fried rice is my personal favorite because it has such a unique and complex flavor profile that is easy to achieve and hard to mess up. Throw whatever ingredients and seasonings your heart desires into the wok along with some rice and soy sauce, and start stir frying!

Recipe sourced from Maangchi, the legend.

Ingredients

For the ingredients, you'll need...

  • 3 cups steamed rice
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi (aged, well-fermented)
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 1/2 cup Spam luncheon meat, chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2-3 tbsp gochujang
  • 3 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon roasted sesame seeds
  • seaweed sheets or laver

Directions

Here's how to make kimchi fried rice!

  1. Heat up the vegtable oil in a wide, flat pan or skillet. Add the kimchi and stir fry for 1 minute over medium-high heat.
  2. Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang. Saute the ingredients together for about 7 minutes, allowing the liquid to reduce down completely.
  3. In a separate pan, fry the chopped spam until crispy on all sides. Once you are done, add the crispy spam to the kimchi fried rice.
  4. Thoroughly mix and stir fry the mixture for an additional 2 minutes and drizzle the sesame oil on top. Remove from the heat.
  5. Garnish with green onion, shredded up roasted seaweed, and sesame seeds. Serve immediately and enjoy!
